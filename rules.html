RULES

Although the game has a lot of complexity to it,
the rules to play it are pretty simple. The game is played where players deliver hand signals that will represent the elements of the game;
rock, paper and scissors. The outcome of the game is determined by 3 simple rules:

  • Rock beats scissors.
  • Scissors beats paper.
  • Paper beats rock.
